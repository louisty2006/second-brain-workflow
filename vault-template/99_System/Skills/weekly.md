## How to Use
```
/weekly
```

Best run on **Sunday evening** or when you feel like reflecting on the week.

## What It Does

1. **Reads** all Daily notes from the past 7 days
2. **Reads** all Inbox captures from the past 7 days
3. **Categorizes** by learning domain
4. **Creates/Updates** proper Learning notes
5. **Identifies** patterns and connections
6. **Moves** processed captures to archive
7. **Generates** a Weekly Synthesis report

## The /weekly Ritual

**What makes /weekly powerful:**

1. **Transforms scattered notes into structured knowledge**
   - Inbox (raw) → Learning (refined)

2. **Discovers patterns you missed**
   - "I ask this question 3 times" = time to go deeper
   - "I have this creative idea repeatedly" = this matters to you

3. **Keeps you aligned with goals**
   - Weekly check-in: "Am I on track?"
   - Prevents goal drift over time

4. **Creates momentum**
   - See what you've accomplished
   - Celebrate small progress
